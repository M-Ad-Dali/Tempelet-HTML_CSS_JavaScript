Yemen Civilization

Yemen is a country with a rich history and civilization, considered one of the oldest in the world. The ancient Yemenis were renowned for their advancements in agriculture, trade, and architecture. It is believed that the descendants of Sam, son of Noah, settled in Yemen, reflecting a strong historical and ethnic connection between the Yemeni people and the Arabs.

The historical significance of Yemen is evident in archaeological sites such as Marib and Shibam. The ancient Yemenis excelled in building entire cities, and Yemen's history spans thousands of years, making it one of the oldest civilizations in the world.
